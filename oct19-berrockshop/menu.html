    Homemade Berrocks

    Our Specialty - delicious fillings wrapped in fresh dough & baked until golden brown. Available individually or they're cheaper by the half dozen & dozen.

    Ground beef - w/ cabbage & onions Shredded roast - w/ cabbage & onions BBQ beef - lean shredded roast w/ tangy BBQ sauce Mexican - seasoned ground beef, two cheeses & mild green chiles Breakfast - scrambled eggs, sauteed onions, cheese...choose ham or bacon

    Our berrocks are large, 8 oz. size and are baked fresh each day. You may special order half size berrocks in any variety, or hors d'oeuvre size berrocks in either the ground beef or shredded roast varieties. Minimum order is one dozen for these special order items and 24 hours notice is required.
